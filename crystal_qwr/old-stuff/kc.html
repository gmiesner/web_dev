ACLU - Kansas

A non-partisan, not-for-profit, membership organization dedicated to the preservation and promotion of civil liberties in Kansas.

ACLU - Missouri

A non-partisan, not-for-profit, membership organization dedicated to the preservation and promotion of civil liberties in Missouri.

BlaqOut KC

BLAQ OUT is a united community of individual advocates, community activists and healthcare professionals who work to address the psychosocial and environmental challenges faced by Black MSM in the Greater Kansas City Area.

Camp Magazine

Kansas City’s voice for the LGBT and allied communities.

Kansas City Anti-Violence Project

KCAVP is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit corporation committed to providing domestic violence, sexual assault, and hate crimes advocacy and education to the lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender and queer community.

Kansas City Center for Inclusion

A LGBT Center that provides a broad array of services for the lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender community. Located at 3911 Main Street in Kansas City, Missouri, The Kansas City Center for Inclusion is a multi-faceted community center and a place for LGBT individuals, their families and friends to call HOME!

KC Legal

Kansas City Lesbian, Gay, and Allied Lawyers is a membership association of the LGBT and allied legal community in the Kansas City metropolitan area.

KC Pride Fest

PFLAG Kansas City

PFLAG Kansas City is a robust local chapter of PFLAG, the nation’s foremost family-based organization committed to the civil rights of lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender (LGBT) people.
