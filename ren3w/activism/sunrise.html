Sunrise in STL

The Sunrise Movement is an environmental movement to stop the negative effects of climate change, but their approach centers traditionally marginalized and ignorned people in public policy. They advocate for a Green New Deal, which will help to stop climate change and create millions of new jobs. The Green New Deal also centers marginalized people, as the organization does itself.

The Sunrise Movement has an active hub in STL. If you get connected with the organization, you can learn more about environmentalism, environmental racism, The Green New Deal, and ways to remain active in the fight against environmental racism and climate change.
