About

This project was created by Isabel Lee, Gabriella Miesner, and Greta Reitenbach over the course of three days at Kode with Klossy. We used HTML, CSS, JQuery, and JavaScript to develop the project and repl.it to collaboratively code.

Renew aims to consolidate information on environmental racism in Saint Louis in an interactive and accessible manner. We based the majority of the information on the site on the WashU environmental racism report, although we plan to greatly expand the website as we do more research. Additionally, our project gives users the oppurtunity to take action and further educate themselves after they learn the realities of environmental racism.
